| [Mid Funnel](Mid_Funnel/index.md) | PDP and Product Consideration / Evaluation | PDP, Offers, Virtual Try-On, Samples |
| [Bottom Funnel](Bottom_Funnel/index.md) | Checkout and Payments | Cart, Checkout, Delivery, Payments |
| [Loyalty](Loyalty/index.md) | Member lifecycle and retention | Points, Tiers, Rewards, Perks |
| [Retail / Omni-channel](Retail/index.md) | In-store and omni-channel experiences | Associate App, Click & Collect, Endless Aisle |
